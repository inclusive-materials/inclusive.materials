Service 01

Consultation

One-on-one expert sessions tailored to your specific situation. Whether you're a parent navigating an IEP, a teacher building an inclusive classroom, or a therapist seeking a second opinion — our consultation service gives you direct access to specialist knowledge.

Each session is focused on your goals, your learner, and actionable next steps you can implement right away.

  • IEP planning & goal-writing support
  • Learner assessment interpretation
  • Classroom and therapy strategy recommendations
  • Program review and recommendations

Service 02

Parent & Teacher Coaching

Practical, ongoing coaching sessions designed to build the confidence and skills of the adults closest to the learner. Coaching is not about theory — it's about giving you real tools you can use today, in the real environment where you work or live.

Whether you're a parent feeling overwhelmed or a teacher supporting a complex caseload, coaching gives you a consistent, knowledgeable partner by your side.

  • Understanding your child's or student's specific needs
  • Behavior management strategies and routines
  • Communication and advocacy skills for parents
  • Differentiated instruction techniques for educators

Service 03

Intervention Support

Direct, structured support for learners who need more than a resource can provide. Intervention support means working alongside the child with a purposeful, evidence-based plan designed specifically for their needs and goals.

Progress is monitored at every stage, strategies are adjusted as the learner grows, and everything stays aligned with their IEP or individual learning plan.

  • Individualized intervention plan development
  • Fine motor, communication, and academic skill support
  • Ongoing progress monitoring and data collection
  • IEP-aligned goals and strategies

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are these services for?

Our services are designed for parents of learners with diverse needs, classroom teachers and Special Education specialists, occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, and other professionals working with children who have learning differences or developmental needs.

Are sessions held online or in person?

Sessions are currently offered online via video call, making it accessible no matter where you are. In-person arrangements may be available depending on location — reach out through our contact page to discuss.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out through our Contact page with a brief description of what you're looking for. We'll get back to you within 1–3 business days to discuss next steps and schedule your first session.

What is the difference between consultation and coaching?

Consultation is a focused, typically one-time or short-term session where you come with specific questions or challenges and leave with expert guidance and a clear plan. Coaching is an ongoing partnership — regular sessions over weeks or months where we build your skills and adapt strategies together as the learner progresses.

Can I combine services with digital resources from the shop?

Absolutely — many clients find that combining personalized services with our downloadable resources gives the best results. During consultation or coaching, we can recommend specific resources from the shop that are the right fit for your learner.

Not sure which service is right for you?

Reach out and tell us about your learner — we'll help you find the best starting point.
