Special Education Resources

At Inclusive Materials, we specialize in creating evidence-informed educational resources designed to support diverse learners and the professionals who work with them.

We understand firsthand how the right tools can transform a student's learning experience. That's why we develop practical, accessible materials — from IEP templates and behavior intervention plans to activity packs and visual supports — designed specifically for Special Education specialists, OTs, SLPs, and educators working in inclusive settings.

Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, ready-to-use resources that help educators do what they do best — support every learner in reaching their full potential.
