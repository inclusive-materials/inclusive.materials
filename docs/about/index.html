About Us

Expert support, evidence-based resources, and a commitment to helping every learner thrive.

Fritz Noreen C. Cabahug

Special Education Specialist

4 Years Experience  ·  M.S. Special Needs Education

Hi, I'm Fritz — a Special Education Specialist with 4 years of hands-on experience working directly with learners with diverse needs. I hold a Master's degree in Special Needs Education, major in Intellectual Disability, from Cebu Normal University.

What drives me is a belief I carry into every session and every resource I create: the world needs to meet these individuals where they are. Learners with disabilities are not less — they simply learn and move through life differently. And when we take the time to understand that, to adapt our approach rather than expect them to conform to ours, they don't just cope — they thrive.

Inclusive Intervention Hub was built on that conviction — to give educators, therapists, and families the tools, guidance, and expert support they need to truly show up for every learner. Because every child has potential. It's our job to help unlock it.
