Privacy Policy

How Inclusive Intervention Hub handles information when you visit or contact us.

Effective date: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Inclusive Intervention Hub (“we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares information in connection with inclusivematerials.com (the “Site”). Please review this policy with legal counsel as needed for your situation.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide. If you use our contact form or email us, we receive whatever you choose to send (such as your name, email address, and message content).

Automatically collected data. Like most websites, hosting and infrastructure providers may log technical data when you visit the Site (for example, IP address, browser type, date/time of requests). We use this information to operate and secure the Site.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to respond to inquiries, operate and improve the Site, analyze aggregate usage patterns where available, comply with legal obligations, and protect our rights and users.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

The Site may use cookies or similar technologies as implemented by our hosting platform or essential services. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-party services

The Site may rely on third-party services (for example, GitHub Pages for hosting, analytics if enabled, payment or storefront providers when you purchase through linked platforms). Those services have their own privacy policies and may process data according to their terms.

5. Children’s privacy

Our Site and resources are intended for educators and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have received such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required by law.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information, or to object or lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise applicable rights, contact us via our Contact page.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures designed to protect information we hold. No method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the effective date above when we do.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through Contact.
